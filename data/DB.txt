# Job Summary:
At Amazon, we aim to be the most customer-centric company on Earth. The Worldwide Delivery Experience (DEX) team is innovating delivery speed initiatives to ensure Amazon remains synonymous with fast delivery in the minds of our customers. We are seeking an experienced Data Engineer to help build scalable solutions that enhance delivery operations and enable high-impact business decision-making.

As a Data Engineer, you will design and develop infrastructure to support data reporting, planning, and control activities. Design, implement, and support scalable analytical infrastructure for large datasets.

Monitor data integrity using automated alerting and dashboards.
